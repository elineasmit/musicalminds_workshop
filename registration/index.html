Conference registration is now open! Registration costs are €75 for senior academics and €55 for (PhD) students. Lunch and coffee will be included on both days. There will be limited space available on the workshop, so please register as soon as you can.

Please click on the following link to register for the workshop: Registration

Deadline for abstract submission is now closed.

The workshop will be a single-stream event and a select number of spoken presentations will be complemented by poster presentations. Authors will be able to select their preference for a spoken or poster presentation during submission.

There will be a student prize on offer for best presentation and poster!

Submitted abstracts are limited to 500 words or less (excluding references) and must follow the following format:
- Introduction
- Aims of the study
- Methods
- Results
- Discussion
- References (if applicable)
