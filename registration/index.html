Abstract submission opens soon, and will be arranged through EasyChair.org. A submission link will be published here soon. The deadline for abstract submission is 14 April 2023 and notifications of acceptance will be sent at the end of May.

Conference registration will open after notifciations of acceptance have been sent out. There will be limited space available on the workshop, therefore registration will open first for accepted presenters.

Submitted abstracts are limited to 500 words or less (excluding references) and must follow the following format:
- Introduction
- Aims of the study
- Methods
- Results
- Discussion
- References (if applicable)
